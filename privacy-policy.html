Broadcast Frequency – Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Broadcast Frequency is a Discord-based game bot. We respect your privacy and want you to understand how your information is used:

  • We collect your Discord User ID, username, and gameplay actions to provide game features and save your progress.
  • No sensitive or personal information (such as email, real name, or IP address) is collected or stored.
  • Game data is only used to operate and improve the bot. Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.
  • If you leave a server or request data deletion, your user data can be erased upon request by contacting broadcastfrequency@gmail.com.
  • This bot is not intended for children under 13, in line with Discord’s own rules.
  • We may update this policy as needed. Material changes will be posted on this page.

For privacy questions, email broadcastfrequency@gmail.com.
