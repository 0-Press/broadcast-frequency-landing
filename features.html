Features & Roadmap

Broadcast Frequency: Echoes of the Fall

What’s Already Here

  • Chat to play: Jump in by typing !transmit [bandit|hunter|medic|survivor] in chat. No install, no login—just type and play.
  • Persistent progress: Vault and currency carry across sessions. Your loot can outlive you, waiting for the next survivor.
  • No “Game Masters,” no manual control: The simulation runs in real-time during every stream session, reporting every event, danger, and loot find directly to chat. (24/7 uptime will arrive with the future cloud server release.)
  • Real-time events: Every tick, something happens: find loot, get injured, heal, starve, or extract with your hard-earned haul.
  • Dynamic zone map: Explore a multi-layered, procedural map—sub-locations and depth matter. The deeper you go, the higher the stakes (and the rarer the loot).
  • Supplies & danger: Manage your supplies, heal injuries, and gamble with the unknown. Extraction is never a sure thing.
  • Corpse system: Every dead player becomes a lootable story for the next wave of survivors. Files are always reused per player—no clutter.
  • Archetypes: Bandit, Hunter, Medic, Survivor—each with their own strengths and style.
  • Inventory, loot, equipment: Hundreds of items—find, equip, vault, or deliver for rewards.
  • Signal strength & milestones: Deliver rare “signal” items to boost your streamer’s channel. Milestones unlock new events. Signal decays over time—keep it charged!
  • Multi-channel support: The game can run in multiple streams at once, with each channel’s zone and survivors tracked independently.

Coming Soon / In Progress

  • OBS Integration: Overlays, alerts, and in-stream effects—customizable and easy to set up. (Launching in a future update.)
  • One-click streamer onboarding: Easy plug-and-play bot connection and settings import.
  • Inter-stream events: Actions in one stream’s zone can trigger events in another. Deliver a signal booster, spark a cross-stream rescue, or call for help.
  • Discover new communities: Encounter survivors from entirely different Twitch channels. Chat can watch, react, and follow the action to other streams.
  • Planned PVP events: Survivors may encounter each other in the zone and compete directly for loot, supplies, or survival. Adds extra unpredictability and excitement.
  • More unique events, anomalies, and rare finds.
  • Expanded archetypes with special abilities.
  • Cosmetic unlocks and persistent achievements.
  • Dynamic environmental hazards, zone mutators, and meta-milestones.
  • Premium Features (Always Optional, Never Pay-to-Win): Cloud saves, custom cosmetics, badges, extra streamer tools. All core gameplay will always remain free to play and watch.

How This Grows Your Stream

  • No grind, no effort: Your chat becomes part of a living world, even when you’re AFK or offline.
  • Drives channel discovery: In-game events create curiosity, cross-channel raids, and organic community growth.
  • Viewers become the story: Every player is a potential legend, hero, or tragic loss—broadcasted to the world, immortalized in the Zone.

Zero Pay-to-Win, Zero Compromises

  • No lootboxes, no microtransactions for survival or power.
  • Premium features are strictly cosmetic or meta, not gameplay advantages.
  • 100% Free during Alpha and Beta. Final release will always have a full-featured free version.
