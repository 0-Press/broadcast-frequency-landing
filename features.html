Features & Roadmap

Broadcast Frequency: Echoes of the Fall

What is Already Here

  • Discord-native hands-off simulation: Join the story by using /transmit and choosing your archetype. All actions and outcomes are fully simulated. Just watch as your survivor's journey unfolds.
  • Persistent progress: Vault inventory and survivor history are cloud-saved for every player. Items can outlive you and your legacy remains in the Zone.
  • No manual control, no grind: The simulation runs automatically, reporting every event, danger, and loot find directly to your Discord channel.
  • Real-time automatic events: Every tick something happens. Find loot, survive danger, heal, extract, or succumb to the Zone.
  • Dynamic zone map: Multi-layered map with sub-locations and increasing risk and reward the deeper you travel.
  • Supplies and danger: Supplies dwindle, injuries happen, and extraction is always a gamble. Every decision is made by the simulation.
  • Corpse and epitaph system: Dead survivors become lootable stories for others. Every death is logged and remembered.
  • Archetypes: Bandit, Hunter, Medic, Survivor. Each has unique event pools, risk and reward, and style.
  • Inventory, loot, and equipment: Hundreds of items. Auto-equipped and managed by the sim. Find, vault, or deliver for rewards.
  • Signal strength and milestones: Deliver rare signal items to boost your server's outpost and unlock new events. Signal decays over time. Keep it charged.
  • Multi-server support: Each Discord server is its own Zone with persistent player state and narrative history.
  • Cloud save for all: Survivor progress, inventory, and death records are saved and recoverable after restart or crash. No premium required.

Development Roadmap

  • Archetype event gating: Unique event pools, actions, and storylines for each archetype. Newbie shield for fresh survivors.
  • Weighted archetype-driven movement: Bandits, Medics, Hunters, and Survivors all travel the Zone differently. Organic weighted pathing, never railroaded.
  • Event and encounter pool expansion: More event templates, rare loot, combat, cross-archetype events, and narrative twists.
  • Survivor history and legacy: Expanded death logs, detailed epitaphs, and survivor cards tracking loot, achievements, and cause of death.
  • Survival, injury, and healing upgrades: Multi-stage danger, injury, death, and intervention events. Gear and event-driven survival modifiers.
  • Inventory and loot rarity expansion: Rare, legendary, and archetype or location-specific loot. More buffs, debuffs, and consumables.
  • PVP engine: Simulated PVP events. Attack, robbery, safe mode for new players, all handled by the simulation.
  • NPC monster boss encounters: Hostile wildlife, mutants, raiders, and more. With archetype and location-based odds.
  • Map and event worldbuilding: More zones, hotspots, rare event spawns, dynamic lore, and evolving zone states.
  • Narrative and flavor expansion: New milestone events, immersive text, and Discord shout-outs.
  • Visuals, web portal, and community: View survivor cards, leaderboards, and death logs on the web. Visual overlays and Discord UI upgrades are optional and modular.
  • Premium and shared world: Premium unlocks cross-server play, world migration, and epic global events. All core gameplay remains free.
  • Security, polish, and scaling: Config lockdown, error monitoring, backend optimization, and anti-tamper safeguards.

No Pay-to-Win. No Grind. Just Story.

  • No lootboxes, no microtransactions for survival or power.
  • Premium is cosmetic, meta, or shared-world only. Never gameplay advantage.
  • 100 percent free during Alpha and Beta. Core game will always have a full-featured free version.
