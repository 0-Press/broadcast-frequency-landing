FAQ

Broadcast Frequency Echoes of the Fall

Q: What is Broadcast Frequency Echoes of the Fall
It is a post-apocalyptic survival simulation for Discord servers. Members join as survivors and the simulation handles all the action. Events, loot, injuries, death, extraction, and more are fully automated. It is hands-off for everyone. Just join and watch your story unfold.
Q: How do players join
Use the /transmit slash command in the server where the bot is active. Choose your archetype and enter the Zone. After that, the simulation takes care of everything and posts updates about your survivor as the game progresses.
Q: What commands can players use
Survival is fully automated, but these core slash commands are available:
  • /transmit - join the simulation and select your archetype
  • /extract - attempt to escape to the outpost
  • /vault - view or bank items
  • /status - check your survivor's status
More commands may be added, but interaction is intentionally limited. This is a hands-off sim where your story unfolds automatically.
Q: Do I control my survivor or take actions each tick
No. All survival, movement, and event decisions are handled by the simulation. You can watch your survivor's journey play out, but you cannot directly influence their actions after transmitting.
Q: Does the server admin have to do anything to run the sim
No manual setup is required after the bot is added. Just start the bot and let it run. The simulation handles everything from events to inventory management.
Q: Will this spam my Discord channels
The simulation only posts important events, discoveries, and survival updates. You can configure which channel is used for sim output. With many players it can get active, but upcoming features will help reduce clutter if needed.
Q: Is there PVP
Not yet. Simulated player-versus-player events are planned for a future update. Right now all threats come from the environment.
Q: How do I get access
The Alpha is free for selected servers and testers. To request early access, contact the dev team or watch for public invite links on the website.
Q: Is this free
Yes. The Alpha and Beta are free for all testers. The final release will always offer a full-featured free version. Premium features for advanced options or cosmetics may be added later but core gameplay remains free.
Q: What is planned for the future
Upcoming updates will include new archetypes, expanded event templates, PVP, shared world features across servers, more loot, web survivor cards, community leaderboards, and more.
